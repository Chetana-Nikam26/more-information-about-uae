more information about UAE

The United Arab Emirates (UAE) is a federation of seven emirates along the eastern coast of the Arabian Peninsula1. It formed in 1971 after British forces withdrew from the Persian Gulf. The UAE is a major center for oil, commerce, and finance in the region1. Here are some key facts about the UAE:

Capital: Abu Dhabi

Official language: Arabic

Official religion: Islam

Time zone: 4 hours ahead of GMT

Currency: Arab Emirati Dirham (AED), pegged to the US dollar (USD) at a rate of 1 USD = AED 3.67252.
